**Post Copy:**
When a crypto holder passes away, their estate faces a valuation challenge that most CPAs and estate attorneys are unprepared for.

Form 706 requires fair market value of all digital assets at the date of death. IRC §1014 provides a step-up in basis for beneficiaries — but only if the valuation is defensible.

For illiquid assets like NFTs and DeFi positions, the IRS expects a USPAP-compliant qualified appraisal.

We just published a comprehensive guide covering:
→ Step-up basis mechanics for inherited Bitcoin, ETH, and altcoins
→ Form 706 reporting requirements for all digital asset categories
